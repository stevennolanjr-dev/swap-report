Boarding actions are authorized but not yet required. The framing as "active economic denial" rather than deterrence marks a formal doctrinal transition. ▲ EARLY — French Rafale deploys laser-guided rocket pods for counter-UAS: first combat verification of cost-imposition approach
AVIATIONIST The Aviationist / OSINT · April 16, 2026 · Cross-corroborated — verified footage
Ground footage from Erbil, Iraq confirms a French Air Force Rafale using laser-guided rockets to intercept Iranian-made one-way attack drones. While positive for port revenue, the surge is stressing chassis availability and truck driver capacity. Port labor contract negotiations resume next week.
Lighter Fare
The Yongbyon timing pattern. Every major U.S. regional crisis since 1999 has been accompanied by a North Korean capability expansion. Social media threads assembling historical infographics are making the rounds. The pattern is consistent enough to feel almost engineered. Social chatter, not a verified single source.
WTI crude down 7% and markets are acting normal. The framing question making rounds in business press: why are markets collectively shrugging at a shooting war? The answer readers are converging on is some version of: everything is priced in except the things that are not. Sentiment observation, not a single-source cite.
The F-47 lore continues to outrun the program. Two years of aviation-Twitter development speculation is now a budget line item. The distance between public mythology and contract reality has rarely been more visible. Commentary, not a cited story.
A 10-day ceasefire brokered by Qatar and France. Two nations with, between them, an exceptional track record of brokering things and a mixed record of those things lasting. Professional optimism in public. Private skepticism somewhat higher. The complaint is not the outcome. It is the durability. Multiple sources.
Jackson County severe weather forecast vs. Altus AFB operations. A perennial Friday tradition. The base, the thunderstorms, and the maintenance schedules have coexisted since 1942. They will figure it out. Local color, not cited.
The CAPE tariff refund portal opens April 20. The most consequential trade policy mechanism of the year launches on a Sunday. Government. Observation.
